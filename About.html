Hello!

I’m Cyrill Acuña and I am an illustrator based in Marikina, Philippines.
I am interested in publishing works and illustrating for brands, music, and stories. My work is inspired on old encyclopedia illustrations and 90’s cartoons.
I enjoy drawing superficial imagery while portraying the mundane moments of most Filipinos.
If you want to work with me shoot me an email aldrincyrill.art@gmail.com :)
